Projects

Robocol

Role: Member
Subsystem: Energy

Robocol is a student initiative in Universidad de los Andes where students from mechanical engineering and electronic engineering are selected because of their skills to work in a prototype for a Mars Rover. We participated in the European Rover Challenge 2019 in Poland, and I took part in the creation and implementation of circuits and energy systems for controlling the traction, arm, cameras, and antennas

Robotics Automation Society Uniandes

Role: President
Subgroup: Minisumo

The Robotics Automation Society (RAS) is a IEEE association chapter focused on implementation and usage of robots. The chapter is currently focused on the design and programming of robots for competitions such as Mini Sumo or Line follower. This year the chapter is looking towards participating in new competitions such as Robot Soccer or Micromouse.
